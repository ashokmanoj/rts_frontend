Step-by-Step Visual Guide

1

Accessing the Login Page

1
Open your browser
Use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge for the best experience.
2
Go to the TELE-RTS portal
Type the portal URL in the address bar and press Enter.
URL: https://telerts.com
3
The login screen will appear
You will see the TELE-RTS login card with the email and password fields.
2

Logging In — Step by Step

TELE-RTS
Tele Education Portal ( Internal Use Only )
✉ Enter factohr email address
🔒 Password
LOGIN
Forgot password?
1
Enter your factohr email address
Click on the Enter factohr email address field and type your registered factohr email.
Example: yourname@factohr.com
2
Enter your password
Click on the Password field and type your password.
Use the 👁 eye icon on the right to show or hide your password.
3
Click LOGIN
Click the blue LOGIN button. You will be logged in and taken to the dashboard.
ℹ️ Important — Use Your Factohr Email
You must use your factohr email address to log in — not a personal Gmail or other email.
Your factohr email is the one registered with your employee account in the HR system.
Format: yourname@factohr.com  (exact format given by HR / Admin)
3

Forgot Password

1
Click "Forgot password?" on the login page
The link is located below the LOGIN button.
2
Enter your factohr email address
Type the same factohr email you use to log in and click Send Reset Link.
3
Check your email inbox
You will receive a Password Reset email from TELE-RTS.
Click the Reset My Password button in the email.
4
Set a new password
Enter and confirm your new password on the reset page, then click Save.
4

Role Selection (Multi-Role Users Only)

If your account is assigned more than one role (e.g., Requestor and HOD), you will see a Role Selection screen after entering your credentials.

1
A list of your roles will appear
Each role card shows the role name and the department it belongs to.
2
Click the role you want to use today
You will be taken directly to the dashboard for that role. You can switch roles later from the top-right profile menu.
Requestor
Submit and track your own requests
Reporting Manager (RM)
Review and approve team requests
Head of Department (HOD)
Department-level approval and oversight
Department HOD
Handle assigned department requests
Management
Full visibility across all departments
Admin
System administration and user management
⚠️ Reset link expires in 60 minutes
The password reset link is valid for 60 minutes only. If it expires, go back to the login page and request a new reset link.
5

Common Login Issues

Issue Possible Cause Solution
Invalid credentials Wrong email or password Re-enter your factohr email and password carefully
Account not found Email not registered in RTS Contact HR / Admin to create your account
Password forgotten — Use "Forgot password?" on the login page
Reset link expired Used after 60 minutes Request a new reset link from the login page
Page not loading Network / browser issue Check internet connection; try clearing browser cache
✅ Need Help?
If you cannot log in after following these steps, please contact your System Administrator or raise a support ticket through the HR department.
